Detailed Description

template<typename SizeT>
struct gunrock::util::CtaWorkDistribution< SizeT >

Description of work distribution amongst CTAs

A given threadblock may receive one of three different amounts of work: "big", "normal", and "last". The big workloads are one grain greater than the normal, and the last workload does the extra work.
